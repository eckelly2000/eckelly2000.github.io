About Us!

Happy Burger: Creating Comfort, Imagining Possibilities

Inspiration

The idea of Happy Burger originated from a simple scribbled line drawing of a “Happy” Burger drawn by a child that immediately resonated joy, pure joy! It became our family reminder to live a joyous life. Enter 2020. Not joyous, for many reasons. Then came Saturday November 7th, a joyous, dancing in the streets day. A day that brought a feeling of hope, a renewal of spirit, and a moment of healing to 2020. Happy Burger seeks to carry these feelings forward. We hope you enjoy the art we create, maybe even get inspired by it, or find a sense of community in it.

Mission

Happy Burger seeks to promote the socially optimistic lifestyle, and to create goods and products that reinforce the tenets of respect, equality, inclusiveness, cooperation and diversity, and hopefully provide some positive impact on our community and our environment.
